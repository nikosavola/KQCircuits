kqcircuits.elements.f2f_connectors.flip_chip_connectors.flip_chip_connector¶

class kqcircuits.elements.f2f_connectors.flip_chip_connectors.flip_chip_connector.FlipChipConnector[source]¶

Bases: kqcircuits.elements.element.Element

Connector between matching faces of two chips.

The connector makes a galvanic contact between the flipped over top chip and the bottom chip. Origin is at the geometric center.

produce_impl()[source]¶

This method builds the PCell.

Adds all refpoints to user properties and draws their names to the annotation layer.

  • bump_diameter (Double) - Bump diameter, default=25, unit=μm
