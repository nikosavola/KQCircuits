kqcircuits.elements.airbridge_connection¶

class kqcircuits.elements.airbridge_connection.AirbridgeConnection[source]¶

Bases: kqcircuits.elements.element.Element

The PCell declaration of an Airbridge with tapered waveguides in both ends.

produce_impl()[source]¶

This method builds the PCell.

Adds all refpoints to user properties and draws their names to the annotation layer.

PCell parameters:

  • with_side_airbridges (Boolean) - With airbridges on the sides, default=True

  • with_right_waveguide (Boolean) - With waveguide on right side, default=True

  • waveguide_separation (Double) - Distance between waveguide center conductors, default=60

  • airbridge_type (String) - Airbridge type, default=Airbridge Rectangular, choices=['Airbridge Rectangular']

  • taper_length (Double) - Taper length, default=31.41592653589793, unit=μm

  • a1 (Double) - Width of left waveguide center conductor, default=10, unit=μm

  • b1 (Double) - Width of left waveguide gap, default=6, unit=μm

  • m1 (Double) - Margin of left waveguide protection layer, default=5, unit=μm

  • a2 (Double) - Width of right waveguide center conductor, default=20, unit=μm

  • b2 (Double) - Width of right waveguide gap, default=12, unit=μm

  • m2 (Double) - Margin of right waveguide protection layer, default=10, unit=μm

  • bridge_width (Double) - Bridge width, default=20, unit=μm

  • pad_width (Double) - Pad width, default=20, unit=μm

  • pad_length (Double) - Pad length, default=14, unit=μm

  • pad_extra (Double) - Bottom pad extra, default=2, unit=μm

  • bridge_length (Double) - Bridge length (from pad to pad), default=48, unit=μm
