Citing¶

You can cite KQCircuits in your projects and publications. Version-specific DOIs and other information in many citation formats, including .bib, are found on Zenodo. Click the badge below to go to Zenodo.

If no reference to a specific version is needed, the following .bib file can be used:

@software{kqcircuits,
  author       = {Heinsoo, Johannes and
                  Inel, Sinan and
                  Janzso, David and
                  Jenei, Máté and
                  Kotilahti, Janne and
                  Landra, Alessandro and
                  Ockeloen-Korppi, Caspar and
                  Räbinä, Jukka and
                  Savola, Niko},
  title        = {KQCircuits},
  month        = jun,
  year         = 2021,
  publisher    = {Zenodo},
  doi          = {10.5281/zenodo.4944796},
  url          = {https://github.com/iqm-finland/KQCircuits}
}
